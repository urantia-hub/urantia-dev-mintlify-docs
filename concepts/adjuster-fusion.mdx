## Overview

Adjuster fusion is the culminating event of a mortal's spiritual career — the eternal, irreversible merging of the human immortal soul with its indwelling Thought Adjuster (a fragment of the Universal Father). This event guarantees eternal survival and creates a being that is both human and divine.

## Key Facts

- **Irreversible** — Once fused, the union is eternal
- **Typically occurs** on the mansion worlds (often the sixth)
- **Rare on Earth** — Very few mortals achieve fusion during physical life
- **Creates** a unique being combining human experience with divine nature
