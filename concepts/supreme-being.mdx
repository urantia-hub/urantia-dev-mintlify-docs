## Overview

The Supreme Being is the experiential God of evolution — a deity who is not yet complete but is growing and evolving through the collective experiences of every creature in the grand universe. Unlike the eternal Universal Father, the Supreme Being is in the process of actualizing, and every mortal's growth contributes to his emergence.
