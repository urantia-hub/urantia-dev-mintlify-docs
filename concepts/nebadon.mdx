## Overview

Nebadon is the local universe in which Earth (called "Urantia") resides. It was created by the Creator Son, Michael of Nebadon, who incarnated on Urantia as Jesus of Nazareth. Nebadon contains approximately 10 million inhabited worlds and is administered from its capital sphere, Salvington.
