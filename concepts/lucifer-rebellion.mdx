## Overview

The Lucifer Rebellion was a cosmic insurrection led by Lucifer, the System Sovereign of Satania (our local system of worlds), approximately 200,000 years ago. The rebellion challenged the existence of the Universal Father and the authority of the Creator Son, affecting 37 inhabited worlds including Earth (Urantia).
