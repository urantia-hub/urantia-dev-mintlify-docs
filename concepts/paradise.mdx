## Overview

Paradise is the eternal, stationary Isle at the geographic center of infinity and the dwelling place of the eternal God. It is the largest organized body of cosmic reality in the master universe and serves as the absolute material-gravity center, the source of all physical energy, and the pattern for all creation.

## Key Facts

- **Motionless** — Paradise does not rotate or move through space
- **Three domains:** Upper (spiritual), Peripheral (functional), Nether (energetic)
- **Not in space** — Paradise exists at the center of space but is not itself in space
- **Destination** of all ascending mortals
