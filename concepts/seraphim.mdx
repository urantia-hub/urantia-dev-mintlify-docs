## Overview

Seraphim are the conventional angels of the Urantia Book, created by the local universe Mother Spirit. They serve as guardian angels, planetary supervisors, and universe ministers. Guardian seraphim are assigned to individual mortals and play a crucial role in the survival and transport of human souls after death.

## Key Facts

- **Created by** the local universe Mother Spirit
- **Guardian pairs** — Seraphim serve in pairs as personal guardians
- **Transport function** — They carry the morontia soul to the mansion worlds after death
- **Multiple orders** serving different functions across the universe
